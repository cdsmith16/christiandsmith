¡Bienvenidos!

My name is Christian Smith and I am an artist, developer, and student of life. I enjoy learning how to do things, understanding how things work, and helping others reach (what I perceive to be) an out-of-cave knowledge of such things. On occasion, I create hip-hop and consume hip-pop. Currently, I am unsure whether it is high time that I return to the cave and labour on with the others, or if I'm still trying to understand the shadow puppets myself. Either way, I owe my entirety to family, friends and educators who gave me awareness, kept me company, and kept the cave warm and well-ventilated. I do not believe in analogical breakdown.
